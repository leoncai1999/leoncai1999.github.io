College Fit For Me Web App

  • Languages and Tools: HTML/CSS, JavaScript, React, AWS, Flask, PostgreSQL, Postman
  • GitLab URL: gitlab.com/parth.shah2/web-project

College Fit For Me is a Web App that allows students to browse information on colleges, their surrounding areas, and attractions nearby. Universities, cities, and attractions can be filtered by a variety of attributes with data retrieved from a custom built API. Additionally, the site shows D3 visualizations of patterns in particular attirbutes.
